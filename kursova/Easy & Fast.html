Easy recipes for students

Here you can find more about the idea behind this site and meet the team

The idea for this site originated during a conversation with my friends, many of whom live independently in Sofia. One common challenge they faced was the difficulty of preparing homemade and tasty meals while juggling studies, work, and a social life without spending a fortune. That's when Easy&Fast was conceived. It serves as a platform where users can discover recipes while not troubling for the availability of products in Bulgaria.

Erik Antov - CEO & Founder
